The main goal of the DISCIce-AQ is:

To characterize air quality and ice mass and thickness in polar regions to collect critical data of correlation between pollution concentration and ice mass and thickness.

Therefore, the goal of our mission goes hand-in-hand with what NASA is trying to accomplish.
