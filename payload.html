Payload


The P-3C Orion will be carrying various instruments designed for collecting data. One of these instruments includes the Atlas sensor. This sensor has a three beam laser that will be used on ICESat-II and has improved elevation estimates. Other instruments that will be used consist of: DACOM, AVOCET, ACAM2, DLH, P-3C Strobe Data System, Snow Radar, and MCo RDS.
