Educational Opportunities


There are many educational opportunities for this mission.

  2. Follow us on twitter: @DISCIce-AQ. By doing so, you will receive updates on the mission and have opportunities to participate in twitcams where you will have the opportunity to ask questions directly to the NASA scientists.
